About Us

The best stories in life have yummy endings.

We opened our first restaurant in the summer of 2019 in Hyderabad! Over a decade and multiple locations later, we has turned our passion for fresh, high-quality deep dish & thin crust pizza and genuine hospitality into a signature experience that can only be summed up as “Lazy's Pizza.”.

Lazy’s embodies what your favorite neighborhood restaurant should be a warm, welcoming place where you can share a delicious meal with friends and family and feel right at home. As we continue to grow and bring the Patxi’s experience to more communities across the country, we’ll stay true to our roots and never forget what makes each bite of our pizza so special. When you walk through our doors, you’re in for a treat!.

When you sit down at our table, you’re family!.
